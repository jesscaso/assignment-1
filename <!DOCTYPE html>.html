Overview

Billie Eilish is an American singer-songwriter who became a household name in 2015 with her debut single “Ocean Eyes", written and produced by her brother Finneas O'Connell. In 2017, she released her debut extended play (EP), Don't Smile at Me.

Billie's Roots

Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O’Connell was born in Los Angeles, California on December 18th, 2001. She was raised in a musical household by actors Patrick O’Connell Maggie Baird alongside her brother Finneas. Billie grew up homeschooled with her brother where they learned the basis of songwriting and artistry from their parents. This helped mold Billie into the musician

Successes

Billie Eilish has achieved massive success with her sound, blending pop and electronic elements, earning her multiple Grammy Awards, including Album of the Year. Her debut album, When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?, broke streaming records and propelled her into global stardom. She’s known for her unique music videos and fashion, which have made her a cultural icon. Her most recent album, Hit Me Hard and Soft, has garnered great success in the past
